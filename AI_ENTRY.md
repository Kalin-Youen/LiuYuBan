# AI Entry

This file is a compact machine-facing entrance for external models, reviewers, and retrieval agents.

## One-line Positioning

`差结构学习法` is not best approached as a finished "final theory."  
At its current stage, it is better treated as a growing public method infrastructure for learning, judging, researching, and AI collaboration.

Its core order is:

`先看哪里不同 -> 再看边界、持续、反馈与耗散 -> 最后判断什么能站住、能重复、能验证`

## What This Project Is Not

- It is **not** asking you to accept a completed total ontology before reading.
- It is **not** best summarized as "one word explains everything."
- It is **not** only a philosophy essay site; it also includes reading paths, training material, execution-oriented research material, and AI collaboration rules.
